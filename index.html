ABOUT ME

Broadcast & Digital Media Specialist | Founder of Sodus Media

I’m a versatile media professional with a strong foundation in television production, product design, and digital journalism. Whether I’m freelancing as a TV producer, UX/front-end product designer, or news blogger, I bring creativity, strategy, and technical precision to every project. My skill set includes: Creative writing, video editing, and content development UX design and frontend development with coding knowledge Team leadership and mentoring with moral excellence

As the founder and creative lead of Sodus Media, I specialize in personal branding through digital visibility. Our mission is to help individuals shape how they’re perceived online because your name should speak for you. Through our signature hashtag #GoogleYourName, we encourage people to search their name and take control of their digital footprint. At Sodus Media, we focus on:

-Optimizing Google Search results to reflect your authentic professional identity.

-Using AI-generated overviews and search tools to reveal how your name appears across platforms.

-Creating digital media content that aligns with your values and connects with your audience.

My core values are brand authenticity and ethical standards guide every project I take on. If you’re looking to create something that stands out and truly connects, I’m here to make it happen.
You are welcome to my world, 😊.
